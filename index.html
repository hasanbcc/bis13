Mission & Goal

  We Burnside Medical Healthcare do comprehensive medical treatment thorough physical exam. We do all kind of blood and urine work, like cholesterol, sugar/diabetes. We check your kidney, liver, prostate. We also check for blood pressure, thyroid hormones, testosterone, and female hormones. In addition, We do EKG, circulations or blood flow to your legs through a test called PADNet.

  Our staffs are very welcoming, patient friendly and do their best to make the patients happy and meet their needs in very professional manners. For a healthier and stronger tomorrow we do preventive cancer screening, like mammogram, refer patients to GYN for pap test and pelvis and breast exam, osteoporosis screening, vision and hearing test, prostate exam. When it is beyond our capacity we refer patients to orthopedics, GIs, cardiologist, pulmonary specialist etc. as required by individual patients medical requirement.

  So, if you suffering from a medical conditions, or its been a while you did not visit a doctor, please mail us for an appointment and blood work, so you can put your body and health in the right track.
